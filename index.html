About Me

I'm a Business and Data Analyst with expertise in transforming complex data into actionable insights that drive business growth. With a background spanning financial technology and entrepreneurship, I've contributed to business development and analytics initiatives at Emergent Payments, Mojo Payments, and as a Business Wizard Co-op on the Entrepreneurship Team at Northeastern University's Roux Institute.

Originally from Accra, Ghana, I hold a BA in English and Political Science and an MSc in Development Finance from the University of Ghana. I'm currently pursuing an MPS in Analytics at Northeastern University's Roux Institute. Based in Portland, Maine, I combine analytical rigor with community engagement, having volunteered my data skills with organizations like the Maine Locker Project.

My work is guided by a commitment to lifelong learning, innovation, and leveraging data to solve real-world problems.
